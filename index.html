Take Action NOW

By firing Jeff Sessions and removing Rod Rosenstein from oversight of the Special Counsel's investigation, President Trump has crossed a red line. Congress must act NOW to protect the integrity of the Special Counsel and the rule of law.

And they'll only act if we do.

In-Person

If any of your lawmakers are holding a town hall meeting or other public event — attend and demand they take action. Ask them specifically: will you support the Special Counsel Independence and Integrity Act?

If they do not have a town hall meeting scheduled on any upcoming weekends or the next congressional recess, call and insist that they do. It’s their job to listen to their constituents.

And if they won’t hold a town hall, take the town hall to them by organizing an Office Visit with other members of your community. Want to have even more impact? Make that Office Visit a lasting one by turning it into a Sit-In.

Call

If public meetings and district offices are distant — and even if they aren’t — you can make your voice count in an instant by picking up the phone. Call and ask your lawmaker's staff if their boss supports the Special Counsel Independence and Integrity Act.

UPDATE

If your member of Congress goes on the record Supporting or Opposing the Special Counsel Independence and Integrity Act please let us know and we'll update this resource.
